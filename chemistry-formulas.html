Chemistry Formulas

Molality
m = moles of solute / mass of solvent (kg)
Molarity
M = moles of solute / volume of solution (L)
Raoult's Law
P = P₀ × X
Nernst Equation
E = E° - (0.0591/n) log Q
Gibbs Free Energy
ΔG = -nFE
Rate Law
Rate = k[A]^m[B]^n
Half-Life (1st order)
t½ = 0.693 / k
Integrated Rate (1st order)
ln[A] = -kt + ln[A]₀
Zero Order Half-Life
t½ = [A]₀ / 2k
Nth Order Half-Life
t½ ∝ 1 / [A]₀ⁿ⁻¹
Kc Expression
Kc = [products]^n / [reactants]^m
Kp and Kc Relation
Kp = Kc(RT)^Δn
ΔH Formula
ΔH = ΔU + PΔV
ΔG Formula
ΔG = ΔH - TΔS
de Broglie Wavelength
λ = h / mv
Photon Energy
E = hν = hc/λ
Bohr Radius
rₙ = 0.529 × n² / Z Å
Bohr Energy
Eₙ = -13.6 × Z² / n² eV
